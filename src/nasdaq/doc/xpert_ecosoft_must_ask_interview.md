# Must-Ask Interview Questions for Fintech Tech Roles

These questions are tailored for candidates interviewing at Xpert Fintech Ltd & EcosoftBS Ltd, focusing on software engineering and mobile app development in the fintech sector.

---

## Technical & Product Questions

1. **Describe your role and technical contributions in developing mobile banking or e-wallet applications. What challenges did you face integrating financial APIs (like MFS, bills pay, QR payments)?**

2. **How have you ensured security and compliance in your fintech apps, particularly around user authentication, data privacy, and regulatory requirements?**

3. **Explain the Clean Architecture and MVVM patterns in your recent apps. Why are these important for scalable fintech products?**

4. **Can you walk through your approach for optimizing app performance and reliability, especially under high transaction loads or real-time market data?**

5. **Have you worked on BO account management or stock market trading features? What are the key technical challenges and how did you solve them?**

6. **Tell us about a time you refactored legacy code in a fintech application. What improvements did you make and what was the impact?**

---

## Programming & Problem Solving

7. **Given your competitive programming background, how would you design a system for processing millions of transactions per day while ensuring accuracy and speed?**

8. **How do you approach debugging and troubleshooting production issues in live fintech apps? Share a specific experience.**

---

## Collaboration & Teamwork

9. **Describe your experience working with cross-functional teams (designers, QA, backend engineers) to deliver a major fintech app feature. How did you handle conflicts or differing priorities?**

10. **What steps do you take to stay current with the latest fintech technologies, security standards, and regulatory changes in Bangladesh?**
